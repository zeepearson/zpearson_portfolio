01 About Me

I'm an Embedded Software Engineer with a passion for building reliable, efficient systems at the intersection of hardware and software. My work spans bare-metal microcontroller development to complex RTOS-based architectures, with a strong focus on real-time constraints and hardware-software co-design.
When I'm not writing firmware, I enjoy exploring new protocols, experimenting with digital circuit design, and contributing to open-source embedded tooling. I believe in writing clean, well-documented code that stands the test of real-world deployment conditions.
